About Me

I bridge the gap between complex theoretical architecture and high-impact market reality. With a Master’s degree in Computer Science, I bring a “system-first” mindset to every codebase I touch.

Currently, I lead the technical implementation for a major Norwegian Children’s Clinic platform, focusing on mission-critical third-party integrations—connecting laboratory results, radiology findings, and national population registries to ensure seamless data flow in high-stakes pediatric care.

I specialize in building enterprise-grade applications by combining robust .NET backends with dynamic TypeScript frontends. As a firm believer in Domain-Driven Design (DDD) and Clean Architecture, I write secure, testable, and maintainable code. I leverage modern AI tools like GitHub Copilot and Claude to accelerate development without compromising architectural integrity.
